About BandBunk

BandBunk was built for the bands that drive four hours to play a support slot, get paid nothing, and can't afford to drive four hours home in the same night.

It was built for the fans who know every word to every song, who've followed bands around the country, and who have a spare sofa just sitting there unused on gig nights.

The UK rock and metal scene runs on passion, not money. Venues are closing. Promoters are tightening. Bands are spending more on fuel and floor space than they'll ever make back at the door. Something had to give.

BandBunk is dead simple: fans post what they have, bands book what they need, everyone pays and gets paid fairly, and both sides hold each other accountable through honest reviews. No algorithms deciding who's "worth it." No hotels. No van floors. Just the community keeping itself alive.

£10. A bed. A brew. A slice of toast. Keep the scene on the road. 🤘
