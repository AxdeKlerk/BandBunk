Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

BandBunk collects the minimum data needed to operate the platform: your name, email, account type, social profile links (for artist verification), and booking details including gig venue information.

Your full address is only shared with confirmed bookings — it is never publicly visible. Payment data is handled entirely by Stripe and is never stored on BandBunk servers.

Reviews submitted are public and attributed to your username. Profile photos and bios are public.

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not run advertising. For data requests or deletion, contact us.
